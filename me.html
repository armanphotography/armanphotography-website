ABOUT

ARMAN FAIZUR RAHAMAN

Arman started his expedition at the age of 12. At the beginning he learned photograhy by watching his dad, who had a hobby of photography. Later, when his dad brought a new camera, Arman started to use his dad's old camera. Initially, he clicked pictures of ordinary things at home. Later on, he found his passion in clicking photos of birds on his terrace and clicking people walking on the streets. He is still an ameatuer photographer and trying to learn more and more everyday.
